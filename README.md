# Axio DO Calculator

- **Instant Calculations:** Derives exact Loan Amount, DBD Charges (1.77%), and Processing Fees immediately.
- **Collect from Customer:** Shows exact cash to collect at the desk (Base Down Payment + Processing Fee + DBD).
- **Shop Total Sale:** Automatically calculates `Total Cash Selected + Net Disbursement` to verify the exact phone price realization.
- **Extra Amount Input:** Allows custom price additions/differences that instantly reflect in the total cash collection string.
- **100% Offline (PWA):** Install to home screen on iOS/Android; works without internet.
- **Auto-Rounded Numbers:** No decimals displayed, zero friction for quick store calculations.
